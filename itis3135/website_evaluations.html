Appleâ€™s website is a prime example of effective design that adheres to the CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: Uses high contrast between text and background for clear readability.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and layout patterns creates visual coherence.
  • Alignment: Elements are neatly aligned, contributing to a structured and professional look.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together, enhancing usability and focus.

LingsCars

LingsCars is known for its cluttered and chaotic design, which fails to effectively apply CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: Color choices clash and provide insufficient contrast, making text hard to read.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent design elements lead to a disjointed and confusing appearance.
  • Alignment: Elements are poorly aligned, resulting in visual disarray.
  • Proximity: Information is scattered, with related items not grouped together effectively.
