research

I am focused on cyber research that applies data mining and machine learning models to network security. My work explores how botnets function, how they spread across distributed systems, and the risks they create for organizations. I use methods such as linear regression, classification models, and clustering techniques to analyze malicious packets and traffic patterns. By applying these approaches, I aim to develop better ways to detect threats, understand attack behaviors, and create stronger defenses against large-scale attacks like DDoS.
