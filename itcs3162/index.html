Research Focus

My research focuses on applying data mining and machine learning techniques to cybersecurity problems. Specifically, I study how botnets operate, how they propagate across distributed systems, and the risks they pose to modern organizations.

I work with methods including linear regression, classification models, and clustering techniques to analyze malicious packets and network traffic patterns. The goal is to improve threat detection, understand adversarial behavior, and strengthen defenses against large-scale attacks such as DDoS.
